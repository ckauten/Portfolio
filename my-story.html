My Story

  • Hi, I’m Cameron. My journey has taken me from the adrenaline of being a Firefighter Paramedic to the advanced logic of web development. It might seem like a shift, but the truth is, a knack for problem solving is universal. In the heat of an emergency scene or the cool focus required for coding, I've found my groove in translating challenges into solutions. The skills picked up along the way—like quick thinking, effective communication, and a strong team spirit—are invaluable assets in the tech world. I’m here to make tech work harder so we don’t have to, leveraging technology to create seamless, user-friendly experiences!

    At home, I have an amazing girlfriend named Jordan and 3 dogs (Tater, Mello and Po). In my freetime, I enjoy spending time with them, riding motorcycles, making music and playing video games.
